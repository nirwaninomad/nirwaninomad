Sit with it.
Let your sorrow twist in the chambers of your heart
With all the hushed ache and silent questioning.
In the stillness, dare to meet your wounded self
No numbing lullabies, no rapid escapes,
Just you and the unfiltered truth of longing.
Sit with it.
Let the shadows speak their wisdom in the quiet;
Their voices know where you've been hurt.
Stop for sometime, The moment you cease to run, you learn to listen,
And in that patient hearing, find the faint glow of hope
A gentle torch that guides you beyond the pain.
Sit with it.
Feel how the broken pieces ache for your empathy,
Feel the tightness of your chest and
The deep intense pain you are escaping so far
It might be disturbing,
You would like to run away and find distraction, But don't
Cradling each jagged edge in compassion.
You mend by daring to remain,
Witnessing your grief transform into strength,
Each tear baptizing you into deeper wisdom.
So feel it and feel it deep inside your abyss
Sit with it.
And from that tender vigil grows a flowering of self,
Its roots drawing from the very heartbreak you feared.
Breathe in, breathe out, and trust the process:
Where you are shattered, you are also opened
And where you sit, you can finally stand tall again.
~ निर्वाण Nomad
